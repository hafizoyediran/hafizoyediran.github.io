Publications

My research has resulted in numerous publications across high-impact journals and conferences in construction engineering, robotics, and information technology. My work focuses on bridging the gap between theoretical research and practical applications in the construction industry, particularly in the areas of robotics integration, safety systems, and Building Information Modeling.
