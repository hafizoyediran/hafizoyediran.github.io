About Me

Brief Bio

I am a Lecturer in the Department of Civil Engineering at the University of New Haven's Tagliatela College of Engineering. I am completing my Ph.D. in Construction Engineering and Management with a minor in Computer Science at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln, advised by Dr. Kyungki Kim. My dissertation, "Robot Integrated 4D Building Information Modeling for Construction Project Planning," explores innovative approaches to construction automation and safety.

Research Interests

My research focuses on construction robotics, 4D BIM integration, and safety management systems for emerging construction technologies. I specialize in ensuring safety verification and automation for robotic systems in construction environments, utilizing expertise in machine learning and Building Information Modeling.
